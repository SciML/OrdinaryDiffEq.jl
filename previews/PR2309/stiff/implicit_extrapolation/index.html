OrdinaryDiffEq.OrdinaryDiffEqExtrapolation.ImplicitEulerExtrapolation — Type

ImplicitEulerExtrapolation: Parallelized Implicit Extrapolation Method Extrapolation of implicit Euler method with Romberg sequence. Similar to Hairer's SEULEX.
